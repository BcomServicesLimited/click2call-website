About Click2Call

Click2Call is an Australian business communications company providing AI-enhanced Cloud PBX, VoIP, and SIP Trunking services to businesses of all sizes across Australia. We are operated by Bcom Services Pty Ltd (ABN: 92 636 893 108).

What Is Click2Call?

Click2Call is a modern, cloud-based business communications platform built specifically for Australian businesses. We provide everything a business needs to manage its phone communications — from a full-featured Cloud PBX with auto-attendants and call recording, to AI-powered voice tools that transcribe, summarise, and analyse every call.

Unlike large global providers, we are focused entirely on the Australian market. This means our products are designed around the Australian telecommunications landscape, our support team is based in Australia, and our infrastructure is hosted locally for optimal performance and data sovereignty.

Our Mission

"To give every Australian business access to enterprise-grade communications technology that is simple, reliable, and intelligently powered by AI."

We believe that powerful business communications should not be reserved for large corporations with big IT budgets. Our mission is to democratise enterprise-grade phone systems for Australian SMBs — making them accessible, affordable, and easy to manage.

Why We Built Click2Call

We built Click2Call because we saw too many Australian businesses struggling with outdated, overpriced, and overly complex phone systems. Small and medium businesses were being forced to choose between expensive enterprise solutions with features they did not need, or basic consumer-grade services that lacked the reliability and features their businesses demanded.

We believed there had to be a better way. By combining the reliability of a carrier-grade network with the intelligence of modern AI and the simplicity of a well-designed product, we set out to build the phone system we always wished existed for Australian businesses.
