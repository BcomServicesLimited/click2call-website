## Website Content & Conversion Audit: Recommendations

Based on a full audit of the existing Click2Call website and a review of key Australian competitors, here are the top recommendations for new content and improvements to increase customer trust and conversion rates. These are prioritised from highest to lowest impact.

---

### 1. Add a Dedicated "Features" Page

**Priority:** &#128640; **Highest**

**Problem:** The site currently lacks a single, comprehensive page that lists all Cloud PBX features. Features are mentioned briefly on the homepage and the Cloud PBX page, but a potential customer cannot easily see a full checklist of what they get. Competitors like CloudPBX Australia have a very clear "Features" section on their homepage.

**Recommendation:**
Create a new top-level page at `/features` that acts as a master list of all Cloud PBX and AI features. This page should:
- Group features into logical categories (e.g., Calling & Routing, Administration, AI & Analytics, User Experience).
- Use a clear, scannable checklist format.
- For each feature, provide a one-sentence explanation of the benefit (e.g., "**Ring Groups:** Ensure no call goes unanswered by ringing multiple phones at once.").
- Include this new page in the main navigation menu.

**Benefit:** This provides a clear, at-a-glance overview of the product's value, directly addressing a primary user need and improving comparison against competitors.

---

### 2. Strengthen Social Proof & Trust Signals

**Priority:** &#128640; **Highest**

**Problem:** The site has very limited social proof. There are no customer testimonials, reviews, ratings, or logos of well-known clients. The "What Do Click2Call Customers Say?" section on the homepage is a placeholder. This is a major gap compared to competitors, who prominently display trust signals.

**Recommendation:**
1.  **Add a Testimonials Section:** Replace the placeholder on the homepage with 3-5 genuine customer testimonials, including the customer's name, company, and a photo if possible.
2.  **Create a Case Studies Page:** The current `/case-studies` page is a good start but needs to be fleshed out with more detail. Each case study should follow a clear Problem -> Solution -> Result format, with quantifiable metrics (e.g., "Reduced missed calls by 40%").
3.  **Display Trust Logos:** If Click2Call has any industry certifications, awards, or has been featured in media, these logos should be displayed on the homepage. Even logos of well-known customers can be powerful.
4.  **Add an Uptime/SLA Guarantee:** Competitors prominently feature `99.9%` or `99.99%` uptime SLAs. Adding a similar guarantee provides significant peace of mind for business customers.

**Benefit:** Social proof is one of the most powerful conversion drivers. It builds credibility and reduces perceived risk for new customers.

---

### 3. Create a Dedicated "Integrations" Page

**Priority:** &#128293; **High**

**Problem:** The site mentions Microsoft Teams integration, but there is no central page that lists all supported third-party integrations (e.g., CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho; or other business tools like Slack, Xero). Competitors like VoIPLine and CommPeak have dedicated integration pages.

**Recommendation:**
Create a new page at `/integrations` that showcases all supported integrations. For each integration, display the logo and a brief description of what the integration does.

**Benefit:** Many businesses make purchase decisions based on how well a new tool fits into their existing software stack. A dedicated integrations page makes this information easy to find and can be a key differentiator.

---

### 4. Add a Detailed "Number Porting" Guide

**Priority:** &#128293; **High**

**Problem:** A major concern for any business switching phone providers is whether they can keep their existing phone number. The site mentions this is possible but lacks a detailed guide on the process. Competitors like Dialpad and CloudTalk have dedicated, in-depth porting guides.

**Recommendation:**
Create a new help guide under "Getting Started" titled "How to Port Your Existing Phone Number to Click2Call". This guide should cover:
- The porting process step-by-step.
- What information is required (e.g., a recent bill from the current provider).
- How long the process typically takes.
- Answers to common questions (e.g., "Will I lose service during the port?").

**Benefit:** This proactively addresses a major customer anxiety, removing a significant barrier to switching and demonstrating expertise and transparency.

---

### 5. Expand the Blog with More "Problem/Solution" Content

**Priority:** &#128295; **Medium**

**Problem:** The existing blog content is good but focuses heavily on explaining VoIP technology (e.g., "What is a SIP Trunk?"). There is an opportunity to create more content that targets specific business problems.

**Recommendation:**
Add blog posts that address common business pain points and position Click2Call as the solution. Examples:
- "How to Stop Missing Customer Calls After Hours"
- "Is Your Outdated Office Phone System Costing You Money?"
- "A Small Business Guide to Answering Every Call Professionally"

**Benefit:** This content attracts potential customers who are aware of their problem but not yet aware of the solution (i.e., Cloud PBX). It captures them earlier in the buying journey and builds authority.
