Your Calls, Automatically Documented

Every time you finish a call, Click2Call's AI goes to work. It reads the recording, writes out everything that was said, and gives you a quick summary so you know exactly what happened — without having to listen back.

Each call gets a written transcript you can read and search, a mood score out of 10 so you can see if the call went well, a topic tag like "Callback Request" or "Customer Complaint" added automatically, and a 2-sentence summary so you can catch up in seconds instead of minutes.

There's more too. Set up an AI Receptionist to answer your phone and transfer callers automatically. Use AI Speech to create hold messages and greetings by just typing what you want said. And set up call alerts so you get an email if a call goes badly or a specific word gets mentioned.

The Free tier includes call transcriptions, sentiment analysis, transcription summaries, profanity analysis, auto language translation, and voicemail transcriptions — all at no extra cost. The Premium tier adds speaker diarisation (identifying individual speakers by name), the most accurate transcription model, priority processing, and the ability to transcribe historical recordings retroactively.

The AI Receptionist handles incoming calls by greeting the caller, understanding their request using voice recognition, and routing them to the appropriate department or extension. You configure a welcome message, choose an AI voice actor, set timeout and retry behaviour, and define which lines connect directly to the receptionist. It operates at all times and requires no human involvement.

AI Speech lets you generate professional voice recordings directly from text. You type your message, choose a gender and voice actor (options include Alice, Emma, Isabella, Lily, Annmarie, Gracie, Sofia, and more), and click Generate AI Voice. The recording can then be used as a hold message, voicemail greeting, or IVR prompt — no recording studio required.

When a call is recorded, the audio is automatically processed by Click2Call's AI engine. It converts speech to text, identifies speakers, applies punctuation, and generates a searchable transcript. The system also produces an AI summary, tags the call with a category (such as Callback Request or Account Management), and scores the sentiment on a scale of 1 to 10. All of this happens automatically after the call ends.
