Connect ElevenLabs to an Australian Phone Number

How to connect your ElevenLabs AI voice agent to a real Australian phone number using Click2Call's native ElevenLabs integration — with call recording, transcription, and human-in-the-loop oversight built in.

By Royce Clark February 23, 2026

Key Takeaways

  • Native ElevenLabs Profile: Click2Call has a built-in ElevenLabs connection type — no middleware, no forwarding, no third-party SIP provider needed.
  • Real Australian Numbers: Give your ElevenLabs agent a real Australian 02, 03, 07, 08, or 1300 number that callers can dial directly.
  • Human in the Loop: Click2Call records and transcribes every call, then emails you a summary — so you always know exactly what your agent is saying.
  • Continuous Improvement: Feed transcribed call data back into your LLM to train and improve your agent over time.

ElevenLabs makes it remarkably straightforward to build a conversational AI voice agent. But to deploy it as a real business phone number that Australian customers can call, you need a local telecom provider behind it. That's where Click2Call comes in.

Click2Call has a native ElevenLabs connection profile built directly into the platform. There's no middleware to configure, no third-party SIP gateway to manage, and no audio forwarding hacks. You select "Eleven Labs AI" as the connection type for your number, enter the ElevenLabs SIP URI, and the two platforms are connected. Your Australian phone number now rings your ElevenLabs agent.

How the Call Flow Works

The end-to-end workflow is simple and reliable. Here's exactly what happens from the moment a customer calls your number:

  1. 1Customer calls your Click2Call number. This can be a local geographic number (02, 03, 07, 08) or a 1300 number — any Australian number on your account.
  2. 2Click2Call routes the call directly to your ElevenLabs agent via SIP. The native ElevenLabs profile handles the routing automatically using the SIP URI sip:sip.rtc.elevenlabs.io:5060;transport=tcp.
  3. 3Your ElevenLabs agent handles the conversation. The agent listens, understands, and responds using the voice and personality you've configured in ElevenLabs.
  4. 4Click2Call records and transcribes the entire call. While ElevenLabs handles the conversation, Click2Call is simultaneously recording the audio and generating a full AI transcription in the background.
  5. 5After the call ends, you receive an email summary. Click2Call emails you the call transcript and a summary so you know exactly what your agent said to the caller — without listening to the recording.

Setting Up the ElevenLabs Profile in Click2Call

The setup takes less than five minutes. In your Click2Call portal, navigate to Line Preferences and open or create a profile for your number:

  • Set the Connection Type dropdown to "Eleven Labs AI".
  • The Inbound SIP URI Route will automatically populate with sip:sip.rtc.elevenlabs.io:5060;transport=tcp.
  • Ensure Disable Transcriptions is unchecked — this keeps Click2Call's AI transcription running on every call.
  • Save the profile and assign it to your chosen Australian phone number.

That's it. Any call to that number will now be answered by your ElevenLabs agent, with Click2Call handling the recording and transcription in the background.

The Human in the Loop: Why This Matters

Deploying an AI agent to answer your business calls is a significant step. Even the best-configured agents can occasionally misunderstand a caller, give an unexpected response, or handle an edge case poorly. The Click2Call post-call email gives you a critical safety net.

After every call, you receive the full transcript. You can review exactly what your agent said, identify any responses that didn't land well, and intervene if a caller needs a follow-up from a real person. This human oversight loop is what separates a responsibly deployed AI agent from one that runs completely unchecked.

"The transcript email isn't just a log — it's your quality control system. You know exactly what your agent is saying to every caller, and you can step in whenever you need to."

Using Call Data to Improve Your Agent

There's a powerful bonus to having every conversation transcribed and stored. That data is a training resource for your ElevenLabs agent.

Using the Click2Call API, you can pull all your call transcriptions programmatically. Feed this data into your LLM — whether that's Claude, GPT-4, or another model — and use it to identify patterns: questions your agent struggled with, topics that came up repeatedly, or responses that callers found confusing. Then update your ElevenLabs agent's system prompt or knowledge base accordingly.

Example improvement loop:

  1. Pull the last 30 days of call transcriptions via the Click2Call API.
  2. Send them to Claude with a prompt: "Identify the top 5 questions callers asked that the agent didn't answer well."
  3. Use Claude's output to update your ElevenLabs agent's knowledge base or system prompt.
  4. Deploy the improved agent and repeat the cycle next month.

This continuous feedback loop means your AI agent gets measurably better over time — grounded in real conversations with your actual customers, not hypothetical test cases.
