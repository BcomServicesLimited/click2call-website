Viewing Call Recordings

If call recording is enabled on your account, all calls are automatically recorded and stored in the portal. This guide shows you how to find and listen to them.

1

Log in to the portal

Navigate to portal.click2call.com.au and sign in.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 1

2

Navigate to Call Recordings or Call History

Click Call Recordings or Call History in the left-hand navigation menu.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 2

3

Filter by date or extension

Use the date range picker and extension filter to narrow down the recordings you are looking for.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 3

4

Play a recording

Click the play button next to any recording to listen to it directly in the browser.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 4

5

Download a recording

Click the download icon to save the recording as an MP3 file to your computer.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 5

6

Enable recording on an extension (if not already active)

If you cannot see any recordings, call recording may not be enabled. Navigate to Extensions, open the relevant extension, and toggle Call Recording on.

Screenshot placeholder — Step 6
