## Services

Click2Call offers a suite of five core services designed to cover every aspect of modern business telephony.

### Cloud PBX

Click2Call's Cloud PBX is a complete, hosted business phone system that runs entirely over the internet, eliminating the need for on-premise PBX hardware. It is designed for businesses seeking enterprise-grade features without the complexity or cost of a traditional system.

- **Key Features**: Auto-attendant (IVR), call recording, voicemail-to-email, call queues, ring groups, music on hold, call transfer, conference calling, and a user-friendly web portal and mobile app.
- **Who It's For**: Small to medium-sized businesses (1-100 staff), multi-site businesses, and companies with remote or hybrid workforces.
- **How It Works**: Calls are routed through Click2Call's cloud infrastructure to VoIP handsets, desktop softphones, or mobile apps. Users can manage their entire phone system through a web browser.
- **Pricing**: Simple per-item pricing. Cloud PBX Landlines $10.00/month (ex GST). Direct Dial Numbers $5.00/month. PBX Extensions free. Every account includes 2 concurrent call channels; additional channels $3.00/month. Call rates from $0.10/min. No bundled plans. See [Pricing Overview](#pricing-overview).

### AI Voice Tools

This is an add-on service for the Cloud PBX that uses artificial intelligence to analyze and extract insights from voice conversations.

- **Capabilities**: Real-time call transcription, call summaries, sentiment analysis (detecting positive, negative, or neutral tones), and keyword/phrase detection.
- **Use Cases**: Compliance monitoring, quality assurance, staff training, and extracting business intelligence from customer interactions.
- **How It Works**: The AI engine processes call audio in real-time, providing transcripts and analytics in the customer portal. It is specifically tuned for Australian accents and dialects.

### AI Receptionist

The AI Receptionist is a conversational AI designed to be the first point of contact for a business. It goes beyond a simple IVR by using Natural Language Understanding (NLU) to have a human-like conversation with callers.

- **How It Differs from IVR**: Unlike a rigid "press 1 for sales" IVR, the AI Receptionist understands open-ended questions (e.g., "I need to speak to someone about my bill") and can route calls, answer common questions, take messages, or book appointments.
- **Use Cases**: 24/7 call answering, handling high call volumes, qualifying leads, and automating routine customer service inquiries.
- **Benefits**: Ensures no call is ever missed, reduces the workload on human receptionists, and provides a professional experience for every caller.

### Microsoft Teams Calling

This service enables full external PSTN (Public Switched Telephone Network) calling directly from the Microsoft Teams application. It turns Teams into a complete business phone system.

- **How It Works**: Click2Call uses **Microsoft Direct Routing** to connect a customer's Microsoft 365 tenant to the global phone network via its SIP trunks. This is often more cost-effective and flexible than Microsoft's own Calling Plans.
- **Benefits**: Allows staff to make and receive calls from their business number on any device with Teams (desktop, mobile, web). It unifies all internal and external communication within a single platform.
- **Setup**: Requires appropriate Microsoft 365 licensing (e.g., E5 or Teams Phone Standard add-on). Click2Call handles the backend configuration.

### SIP Trunks

SIP (Session Initiation Protocol) Trunks are a modern, IP-based replacement for traditional ISDN or PSTN phone lines. They are designed for businesses that already have an on-premise or private cloud IP PBX system.

- **Compatible Systems**: 3CX, FreePBX, Asterisk, Cisco, Avaya, Yealink, Grandstream, and most other IP-based PBX systems.
- **Benefits**: Significant cost savings compared to ISDN, greater scalability (add or remove channels instantly), improved reliability with multi-carrier redundancy, and geographic number flexibility.
- **Use Cases**: Businesses with existing PBX hardware, call centres, and organizations requiring a high volume of concurrent calls.

- **Internet**: A stable, business-grade internet connection (NBN, Fibre, 5G) is required. A minimum of 100kbps of uncongested bandwidth is recommended per concurrent call.
- **Hardware**: For Cloud PBX, users can use VoIP desk phones (from brands like Yealink, Grandstream), the Click2Call desktop softphone app (Windows/Mac), or the mobile app (iOS/Android). No on-premise server is needed.
- **Compatibility**: SIP Trunks are compatible with any standard IP PBX. Microsoft Teams Calling requires specific Microsoft 365 licensing.
