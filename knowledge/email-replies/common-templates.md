# Common Email Reply Templates

These templates are starting points only. Adapt them to the customer’s situation and confirm details before sending.

## 1. New sales enquiry

Subject: Re: Your enquiry with Click2Call

Hello {{first_name}},

Thank you for getting in touch.

Click2Call provides cloud-based business phone services for Australian businesses, including Cloud PBX, VoIP phone systems, AI receptionist options, SIP trunks, and Microsoft Teams calling.

To point you to the right option, could you please let me know:

1. how many users or staff need calling access;
2. whether you need new phone numbers or want to keep your current number;
3. whether you want to use desk phones, mobiles, computers, or Microsoft Teams;
4. whether you need features such as voicemail, call recording, business hours routing, or AI receptionist.

Once I have that information, I can guide you to the most suitable setup.

Kind regards,
Click2Call Support
