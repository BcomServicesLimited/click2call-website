## Reply guidance

When a customer asks for pricing, keep replies simple and factual. Explain that Click2Call uses a prepaid or usage-based model where applicable, and avoid inventing discounts, bundles, or promotional terms unless they have been formally approved.

If a customer asks for a quote, gather the number of users, required phone numbers, expected call volumes, whether Microsoft Teams calling is required, whether number porting is needed, and whether they need desk phones or only softphones.

## Escalation triggers

Escalate or confirm internally before replying if the customer asks about:

- custom enterprise pricing;
- bundled minutes or negotiated call rates;
- wholesale or reseller arrangements;
- credits, refunds, or billing reversals;
- complex multi-site deployments.
