# Internal FAQ Starters

This file captures common questions that may need a short approved answer in email replies.

## Questions to maintain here

| Question | Starter guidance |
|---|---|
| Do you support Australia-wide customers? | Public website positioning indicates service for Australian businesses. |
| Can I keep my current number? | In many cases yes, subject to provider and service checks. |
| Do I need hardware? | Public-facing material indicates no hardware is required for many use cases, though compatible desk phones may also be supported. |
| Do you offer a free trial? | Yes — a 7-day free trial is available for Cloud PBX with no credit card required. New accounts are loaded with $11 credit and 10 test calls automatically. |
| Is there a lock-in contract? | Public-facing content says services are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts. |
| Do you support Microsoft Teams calling? | Yes, this appears as a core service line on the website. |
| Do you have Australian support? | Public content refers to Australian-based support. |
