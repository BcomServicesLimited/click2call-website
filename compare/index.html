How Does Click2Call Compare?

Choosing a VoIP provider can be confusing. This page provides a factual, balanced comparison between Click2Call and other major providers in the Australian market. Our goal is to give you the information you need to make the right choice for your business, even if that choice isn't us.

Key Takeaways

  • Australian Focus: Click2Call is built for Australian businesses, with local data hosting and expert local support.
  • Integrated AI Features: AI tools like transcription and call summaries are standard, not expensive add-ons.
  • No Lock-in Contracts: We operate on a prepaid credit model with no fixed terms, unlike the multi-year contracts common with larger providers.
  • Transparent Pricing: Our pricing is simple and public, aiming to avoid the complex quotes and hidden fees of competitors.
  • Balanced Comparison: We acknowledge that for large, multi-national enterprises, global providers like RingCentral or 8x8 may offer a broader feature set.

When Another Provider Might Be a Better Fit

  • -Large Multi-National Enterprises: If your business has offices in many countries and needs a single global contract, a provider like RingCentral or 8x8 may be better equipped to handle that scale.
  • -Deep Contact Centre Needs: If you need a full-blown omnichannel contact centre with advanced workforce management, providers specializing in CCaaS may be more suitable.
  • -Bundled Carrier Services: If you are locked into a long-term contract with a major carrier like Telstra and want to bundle all services, their solution might be the path of least resistance.
