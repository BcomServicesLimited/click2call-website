Trades & Services

Sydney Plumbing Business Unifies Three Locations with Cloud PBX

Challenge: A Sydney-based plumbing company with teams across Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane was running separate phone systems at each location. Calls could not be transferred between states, there was no shared directory, and management had no visibility over call volumes or missed jobs.

Solution: Click2Call deployed its Cloud PBX across all three locations under a single account. All staff were added to a central directory, enabling seamless transfers. Call queues were configured to handle incoming job requests, with overflow routing to a shared after-hours team.

"For the first time, we operate as one national business. A client in Perth can be seamlessly transferred to a specialist in Sydney. It's completely changed our workflow for the better."

Melbourne Medical Clinic Improves Compliance with AI Call Transcription

Challenge: A specialist medical clinic in Melbourne needed to ensure its reception staff were providing accurate information and adhering to strict patient privacy protocols. Manually reviewing calls was time-consuming and inconsistent, leaving compliance gaps.

Solution: The clinic added Click2Call's AI Voice Tools to its existing Cloud PBX. All calls are now automatically transcribed and summarised. Keyword detection flags calls where sensitive terms are mentioned, allowing targeted review and quality assurance without listening to every recording.
