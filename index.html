How Does Click2Call Work?

Getting started is straightforward. Most businesses are making calls within a single business day.

1

Sign Up

Create your account at portal.click2call.com.au/join. Choose your plan, select how many seats you need, and pick your phone numbers. The whole process takes about five minutes and requires nothing more than a standard internet connection.

2

Configure

Use the Click2Call portal to set up your IVR menus, ring groups, business hours, voicemail greetings, and call routing rules. Everything is point-and-click — no technical knowledge required. If you need help, our Australian support team is available to assist with configuration at no extra charge.

3

Start Calling

Download the Click2Call softphone app on your computer or mobile, or plug in a compatible IP desk phone. Your team can immediately start making and receiving calls on their existing devices. If you are porting an existing number, calls will automatically switch over on the agreed porting date with no interruption to service.
