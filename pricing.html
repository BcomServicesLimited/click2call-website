How Many Do I Need?

One channel per simultaneous call. A 10-person team rarely needs more than 4–6. Contact us if unsure.

Note: All outbound calls — including those from free extensions — use a channel from your shared pool. If all channels are in use, outbound calls will queue until one is free.
