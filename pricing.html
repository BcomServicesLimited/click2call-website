How Our Prepaid System Works

Click2Call runs on a simple prepaid credit model. There are no monthly invoices or surprise bills — you stay in control of your spending at all times.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in minutes. Your account is free to create — no credit card required to get started.

2

Add Credit

Top up your account balance with any amount — most customers start with $20–$50. Top-up amounts can be as low as $10.

3

Use Your Services

Monthly number fees and per-minute call charges are automatically deducted from your balance. Top up again when you run low.

Important: Your balance can never go below $0. If your credit runs out, outbound calls pause until you top up — inbound calls continue uninterrupted.

Key Takeaways

  • Prepaid Credit System: Add credit to your account first, then your usage is automatically deducted. You can never go below a $0 balance.
  • No Plans or Bundled Minutes: Pay only for what you use — services are deducted from your credit as you use them.
  • All Prices ex GST: No surprise charges — what you see is what you pay.
  • No Lock-in Contracts: All services are month-to-month.
  • 30-Day Free Trial: Test our Cloud PBX risk-free, no credit card required.
  • Australian Support Included: Local, expert support with every account.
  • No Setup Fees: Nothing to pay to get started.
