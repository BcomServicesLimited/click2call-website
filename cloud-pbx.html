What Is a Cloud PBX Phone System?

A Cloud PBX — also referred to as a hosted PBX, virtual PBX, or cloud phone system — is a business telephone exchange that runs entirely on servers in the cloud rather than on physical equipment installed at your office. The term PBX stands for Private Branch Exchange, which is the technology that routes calls between internal extensions and the public telephone network. In a traditional setup, this equipment sits in a server room or comms cabinet on your premises. With a hosted PBX, that same functionality is delivered as a service over the internet, managed entirely by your provider.

The shift to cloud telephony has been driven by the widespread availability of high-speed broadband across Australia. Because voice calls are transmitted as data packets over the internet — a technology known as business VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) — there is no longer any need for dedicated telephone lines or expensive on-premise hardware. Your phone system becomes software: configurable, scalable, and accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.

The difference between a Cloud PBX and an on-premise system is significant in practical terms. An on-premise PBX requires a capital investment in hardware, professional installation, and ongoing maintenance by a qualified technician. Every time you need to add a new user, change a call routing rule, or add a feature, you typically need to call a technician or wait for a scheduled maintenance window. With hosted telephony, all of that is replaced by a web-based portal you control yourself. Adding a new seat takes seconds, and changes to your call routing take effect immediately.

Click2Call's Cloud PBX is built on Australian-hosted infrastructure, which means your voice traffic never leaves the country for domestic calls. This delivers lower latency, better call quality, and compliance with Australian data sovereignty requirements. Whether you are a sole trader wanting a professional phone presence or a multi-site business needing centralised call management, a cloud phone system from Click2Call gives you enterprise-grade capability at a fraction of the cost of traditional telephony.
